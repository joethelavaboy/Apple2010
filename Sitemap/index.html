Collection: University of Michigan Athletics Web Archives
The University of Michigan Athletics Web Archives includes materials related to the university's athletic department and teams. The 2015-16 academic year marked the 150th anniversary of Michigan Athletics and the web archives provide significant documentation of the coaches, staff, and student-athletes. These materials complement and/or supplement additional physical and digital materials related to Michigan Athletics at the Bentley Historical Library.
